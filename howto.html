Introduction

Your employees / sub-contractors will be sitting this induction because shortly they will be working on the fit out of one of the retail units located on the Birmingham Gateway Project (BGP). Their works will take place within the confines of the retail unit known as the demise. Within the demise, they will be working under the control of the lead fit out contractor. This lead fit out contractor will perform the role of Principal Contractor for works carried out within the demise

Access and egress to the demise will be across the BGP site where Mace are the Principal Contractor. This Mace induction that you are registering for, and your employees will carry out is to brief them on the relevant Health & Safety information that they will need to be aware of, whilst gaining access across to the BGP to your demise.

Please note that it is the responsibility of you the employer / lead fit out contractor of your demise, to give your employees a full induction for the works they will be carrying out within the demise.
